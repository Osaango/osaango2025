## Challenge

The organisation needed to renew legacy integrations while keeping critical services running. Documentation was incomplete, ownership was fragmented and the current architecture made change slow.

## What we did

- Used AI-assisted analysis and architectural discovery methods to speed up understanding of legacy systems.
- Mapped integration dependencies, information flows and service ownership.
- Identified modernisation opportunities aligned with target architecture principles.
- Created a practical architecture renewal roadmap for APIs, integrations and platform capabilities.

## Outcome

A clearer path for legacy modernisation, stronger architecture governance and practical priorities for moving toward an API-first and AI-ready integration environment.
