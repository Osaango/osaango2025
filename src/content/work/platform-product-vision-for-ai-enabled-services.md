## Challenge

A global SaaS organisation needed a clearer platform product vision. Customer and partner integrations were increasingly strategic, but the roadmap, platform capabilities and ownership model needed sharper direction.

## What we did

- Clarified customer, partner and internal team needs around integrations and platform capabilities.
- Identified where AI-enabled services and automation could create measurable value.
- Connected product strategy, API architecture and platform operating model decisions.
- Built a sequenced roadmap for platform capability development and governance.

## Outcome

The leadership team gained a clearer platform strategy, stronger prioritisation criteria and a practical path for combining APIs, integrations, data and AI-enabled services into reusable business capabilities.
